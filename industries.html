Industry-Specific Capabilities

Railways Components

We manufacture precision machined components for railway signaling, safety systems, and rolling stock. As an approved vendor to Autometer Alliance Ltd. (associated with Indian Railways), we understand the critical nature of railway parts.

  • Signaling system components
  • Safety interlocking parts
  • Precision housings and brackets
  • RDSO compliant manufacturing

Automotive Components & Jigs

We are a strategic Tier-1 partner to leading automotive wiring harness manufacturers. Our expertise lies in designing and manufacturing circuit check jigs, pneumatic clamp jigs, and assembly fixtures that ensure zero-defect validation.

  • Wiring harness checking jigs (Circuit, Case, Fuse Box)
  • Pneumatic clamping fixtures
  • Air compressor parts (Anest Iwata)
  • Wheel balancing/alignment machine sub-assemblies

Defense & Aerospace

For defense and aerospace applications, we maintain rigorous quality control and full material traceability. Our components are built to withstand extreme conditions and meet stringent performance specifications.

  • High-precision machined components
  • Material certification and traceability
  • Strict compliance documentation
  • NDT and special process coordination

Industrial Equipment

We supply machined components and sub-assemblies for various industrial applications including commercial paint booths, diagnostic equipment, and general engineering.

  • Paint booth components (Spray Technics)
  • Wheel balancer & aligner parts
  • Spot welding machine components
  • Custom industrial parts as per drawing

Three decades of precision, trust, and engineering excellence.

— Since 1987

Engineering Excellence
Built on Trust

What began as a small toolroom in 1987 under the competent leadership of Mr. Rajesh Arora (Mechanical Engineer) has evolved into one of Noida's most trusted names for precision machining.

We are a Sole Proprietorship firm and an ISO 9001:2015 certified company. Unlike mass-production houses, FMI excels at complex, custom-engineered components. Our strength lies in our 70-member skilled workforce who combine the wisdom of conventional machining with the efficiency of advanced CNC automation.

With over 35 years of experience, highly controlled processes, and a robust Quality Management System, we offer both precision and reliability to clients across Railways, Automotive, and Defense sectors.
